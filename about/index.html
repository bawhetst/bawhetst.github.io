About

Hi, I’m Ben Whetstone, a software developer based in the Research Triangle in North Carolina.

I was a bit of a late bloomer in regards to computer science, having had no exposure to it prior to college. I fell in love with the challenge of taking an abstract idea and defining it in terms that both a computer and other humans can understand. That is the fundamental challenge of computer science which means that there will always be something interesting to work on. I believe that if my work doesn’t allow for someone else to build on it, I might as well not do it.

Outside of technology, I’m interested in music (indie, hip-hop, and electronic) and cooking.
